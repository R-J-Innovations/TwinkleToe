Your Peace of Mind

Your Baby's Safety Is
Our First Priority

We know that leaving your baby with someone else is one of the hardest things a parent can do. That's why every decision we make — from our caregiver ratios to our cleaning routines — is designed to give you complete peace of mind.

🛡️

Always Supervised

Your baby is never left unattended. Our caregivers maintain constant, loving supervision — whether awake, eating, playing, or sleeping.

🧴

Spotlessly Clean

Daily sanitization of every surface, toy, and piece of equipment. We maintain rigorous hygiene standards so your baby stays healthy and safe.

👶

Personal Attention

With a maximum of 4 babies per caregiver, your child receives individual attention, responsive care, and genuine emotional connection — every single day.

🔐

Secure Premises

A fully secured, gated facility. Only authorized parents and caregivers have access, and you control exactly who is permitted to pick up your baby.

📱

Daily Photo Updates

We send you photos and messages throughout the day so you always know your baby is happy, fed, and thriving. No news is good news — but we prefer to share.

🏥

First Aid Certified

All our caregivers hold current paediatric first aid certification. We're fully prepared so you never have to worry about emergency readiness.

A Day in the Life

Every Moment Is Meaningful

We follow a gentle, structured routine that honours your baby's natural rhythms. Every part of the day is intentional — designed to nurture development, joy, and a deep sense of security.

6:30 AM
🌅

Welcome & Morning Cuddles

Each baby arrives to a warm, familiar face. We take time for individual greetings, making sure every little one settles in feeling safe and loved from the very first moment of the day.

8:00 AM
🍳

Nutritious Breakfast

A warm, age-appropriate breakfast to start the day right. We follow each baby's individual feeding schedule and dietary needs with careful consistency and care.

9:00 AM
🧩

Sensory Play & Tummy Time

Supervised tummy time, sensory discovery baskets, textured toys, mirrors, and sounds. This is where tiny brains and bodies grow the most — through joyful exploration.

10:00 AM
🌿

Snack & Outdoor Exploration

A mid-morning snack followed by fresh air and sunshine. Outdoor play supports physical development, mood regulation, and the simple joy of feeling the world around them.

12:00 PM
🍲

Freshly Cooked Lunch

A nutritious, home-cooked meal prepared with love. We introduce new flavours and textures in a gentle, supported way — building lifelong healthy eating habits one spoonful at a time.

12:30 PM
😴

Peaceful Nap Time

Individual cots, soft lighting, and gentle white noise. We respect each baby's sleep schedule so they wake up refreshed, content, and ready to explore the afternoon.

2:30 PM
🎨

Creative Arts & Music

Finger painting, music, movement, and imaginative play. These activities build creativity, coordination, and language skills in the most natural and joyful way possible.

4:30 PM
🧸

Story Time & Quiet Play

Calming down with stories, songs, and gentle play as we prepare for happy pickups. Your baby will be content, pleasantly tired, and absolutely ready to come home to you.

Child Development

We Don't Just Care for Babies —
We Help Them Grow

Every activity at Twinkle Toe is intentionally designed around the four pillars of early childhood development. Your baby isn't just being looked after — they're being nurtured into their fullest potential.

❤️

Emotional Security

Consistent, responsive caregiving builds secure attachment. Babies who feel emotionally safe are more confident, curious, and resilient throughout life.

  • Consistent caregiver relationships
  • Responsive to cues and emotions
  • Gentle transitions and routines
🧠

Cognitive Growth

We stimulate curiosity, language development, and problem-solving through play-based learning that feels like pure fun — because it is.

  • Sensory discovery activities
  • Language-rich environment
  • Age-appropriate challenges
🏃

Physical Milestones

Supervised tummy time, movement play, and outdoor exploration support the physical milestones every parent wants to celebrate and cheer.

  • Daily tummy time & movement
  • Fine and gross motor activities
  • Safe outdoor exploration
🤝

Social Skills

Even the youngest babies begin learning social awareness. Being around other babies in a safe, small-group setting builds the foundation for lifelong social confidence.

  • Small group interactions
  • Turn-taking awareness
  • Peer bonding and play
